About

Think you can engage in a battle of wits? Addicted to “observing” every invisible obvious detail? Take a stroll down 221B Bakers Street. This mind boggling maze tests your astute observation skills, deductive reasoning and logical thinking. With Google as your Dr Watson encounter nail biting situations as you find your way through this labyrinth for at the end only one will be crowned SHERLOCK!

Game Play

221B Baker Street is an online treasure hunt and quiz event, which is going to held at official techNITi19 app. It consists of two sub-events, General Mode and Story Mode. Instructions specific to each mode will be displayed on the app prior to commencement of the Mode. Both modes consist of questions in the form of a series of images presented to the participant. The participant has to deduce the answer from said image, taking the help of any and all resources at her/his disposal. The winner will be judged as the person with the most questions solved in the shortest time.

GENERAL MODE :
DATE : 8th February 2019 (FRIDAY).
It consists of some mind-boggling questions, covering all genres, in classic quizzing style, generally to test the participant’s googling skills, problem solving ability general knowledge and creative thinking.

STORY MODE :
DATE : 17 February 2019 (SUNDAY).
It presents questions in more continuous format, with questions linking the story and vice versa, i.e., questions are part of the story itself.

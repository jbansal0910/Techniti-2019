About

Freak-O-Matix is an event related to math and aptitude where you are exposed to different kinds of logical problems. This makes you approach the problem in all directions which is further useful in your future especially interviews. Brush up your brain and bring out your problem solving skills.

ROUNDS

1. Test your skills

a. ONLINE ROUND: The sums posed are based on logic and arithmetic. They are subjective and test your reasoning. This round also provides a small window for us to peek into your brain. 2. Touring VNIT

This is a treasure hunt. Teams must follow the clues provided and search for the objects hidden throughout the campus. First round qualifiers (from online and offline) will be sorted into groups of 6-8 contestants. The hunt will be held on the third day of techNITi19.

3. Surprise round:

The contestants qualified from second round will be participating in this stage. This is a surprise round. This will also be scheduled on the third day of techNITi19.

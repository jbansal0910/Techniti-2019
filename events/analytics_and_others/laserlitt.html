About

Laser litt, an event full of sparkling lasers, shiny mirrors and rugged obstacles where you have to make the way for the laser beam to hit the target with the help of the llaws of reflection and refraction in the limited time frame. This event consists of one elimination round followed by a final round where you would not compete with time but the opponent team.

Event details

Laser Litt is the optical equivalent of what you might call a maze. Immerse yourself into the delight of striking shiny mirrors and bending around rugged obstacles to finally hit your target. In this event you have to make the way for the laser beam to hit the target with the help of the laws of reflection and refraction in the limited time frame. This event consists of one elimination round followed by a final round where you would not compete with time but the opponent team.

Rules and Regulations

  • The participants have to register for Laser Litt on www.axisvnit.org and verify their techNITi19 ID.
  • The team should consist of 2-3 members (max.)
  • The competition would be timing based, the team completing the maze in minimum be time would qualify for 2nd round.
  • 10 teams would be qualified for 2nd round on the basic of timing.
  • The final would be a knockout event.
  • The final decision would be based on the timing basis on 1st and 2nd event.

About

PARADEIGMA is the most outstanding and astonishing event of techNITi19. The event is open for all those who have an unexplained passion for building dynamic models. This mega event is an on-the-spot event for designing and building a model with the raw materials provided. The event basically calls for teamwork, spontaneity and judicial use of raw materials provided.

Problem Statement

The problem statement is what makes this event exciting and different, which will be provided to the participants with the apparatus after they register themselves, on-the-spot !!!

Rules and Regulations

  • Kit provided will be sufficient.
  • No extra material should be used. (if found then team will be disqualified)
  • The event will be conducted in two rounds:
    1. Preliminary round which consists of making a dynamic model from material provided.
    2. Final round in which the model made by teams will compete one on one.
  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 1 and maximum of 2 participants.
  • Participants are allowed to use electronic gadgets if required.
  • Decision of event managers shall be final and binding

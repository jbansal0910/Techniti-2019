About

Have an aptitude for physics? Can you construct your own turbine? Then gear up for the event TURBOFLUX. Time to bring out the Bernoulli in you and use your hydraulics knowledge to rotate a turbine. Decide on the pipe you will use to pass water through all obstacles. Do check for the path that will make water pass with the greatest speed?
All that u have to do is to come up with your own turbine. Have fun while you master!

Problem Statement

The basic applications of fluid mechanics is to be applied where the water is to be progressed from one point to another passing through various obstacles so that it falls over the turbine with sufficient velocity,rotates it and thus producing significant electricity. Knowledge of high school fluid mechanics and mere capability of application of them are just its prerequisite.
Think…Which pipe will you prefer V-shaped or a U-shaped for flow of water??

Gameplay


Day1:
  • Registering for the event at the registration desk.
  • Selection of obstacles and placement of pumps for the execution later next day.
  • Can give an offline test consists of basics of Fluid mechanics to get bonus points.
  • Allotment of time slots for execution.
    Day 2:
  • Executing the corresponding set-up selected by the participant on the previous day using the turbine made by them.
  • Execution process will be done according to the time slots allotted on Day 1.

    • Rules and Regulations


    Rules
  • Every participant must register at www.axisvnit.org.
  • Maximum Number of participants per team: 3
  • Participants should come with a turbine.
  • The obstacles will be disclosed on the day of the event and the sequence of the obstacles should be decided by the participants.
    NOTE: In case of any dispute, techNITi19 Executive Committee’s decision will be final and binding.
    Turbine specifications:
  • Diameter of turbine should be less than 40 cm
  • Bore diameter should be 6 mm.
  • There is no restriction regarding type of material you use for making turbine.
  • Weight of turbine should not be more than 800g.
  • The turbine should be strictly to the specification we have provided. Deviation from it may cause disqualification.
  • The arrangement of the obstacles and the pumps on the arena as well as mounting of the turbine will be done by the organizers.
  • The participants will be allowed to control the time at which they want the pumps to be switched on.
  • Once the sequence of obstacles is decided, no changes will be allowed.
  • Bonus points will be there for using lesser number of pumps than allowed.
  • Bonus points will also be there for giving the test on Day1.

    • Criteria Of Judgement

  • Voltage generated due to rotation of turbine.
  • Time water takes from start to reach to turbine.
  • Meanwhile, other minor criteria will be revealed that day itself.
  • The team with the most innovative turbine will be given extra points.
    Note: In case of any disputes, the decision taken by the techNITi19 Executive Committee will be final and binding.

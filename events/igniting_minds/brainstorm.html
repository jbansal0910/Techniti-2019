About

Brainstorm the Annual Quizzing episode of techNITi19, VNIT Nagpur challenges you to battle it out against the best quizzing minds in the country. Show your hunger for knowledge your thirst for the trophy and put on your grey cells to work as we bombard your with amazing trivia and never heard before facts. Do you have the wit and knowledge to challenge anyone with your sports, history, literature, science and entertainment knowledge?. If yes, then what are you waiting for?

  2. Number of participants per team should be 2.
  3. Registration fees per team is Rs. 50
  4. Your registration will only be confirmed only if payment is done for the event.(Please refer PAYMENT POLICY to do the payment).
  5. After payment you will receive a confirmation mail about your successful registration within 48hours.

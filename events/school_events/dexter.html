Event Details

"Talent hits a target no one else can hit; Genius hits a target no one else can see.” Do you think you possess the ability to hit the bull’s eye of invisible targets? Can you find answers where others can’t see any? We bring to you an event that will push you to your limits and make you explore the genius residing inside you. DEXTER is not just an aptitude test. It is a journey that puts your intellectual as well as interactive skills to test. Taking forward the huge success from last year, we present to you an opportunity to identify your skills. We will encourage and help you to enhance your endowments. Through DEXTER, we aim to not just find an intelligent kid, but to find and help groom a complete individual equipped with all the tools required to excel in this competitive world. Students will learn to open up their minds, think out of the box, and gain a whole new interactive experience. Also, a healthy competitive spirit will be inculcated among the participants. techNITi19 aims to slightly nudge the dexterity that resides in these minds and to provide a platform for them to showcase their competence, thus taking a step towards innovation and excellence. DEXTER is a nationwide aptitude and logical reasoning test for classes VIII-XII which assesses dexterity, logical reasoning and mental ability. It is carried out in 2 rounds.

Game Play

Round 1:
A written test was conducted across India and abroad from 11th – 29th of December in which students from all over India participated. Round2:
Top performers of round 1 will be selected and called for round 2 which will be held at Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur. It will be an interactive session which will include assessment based on management skills, aptitude, creativity skills and communication skills. Round 2 will be held during techNITi19 and hence student can witness techNITi19 live.

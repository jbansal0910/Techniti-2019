Introduction

As you walk through this door, stretch and flex all of your neurons because you’re going to find yourself sinking into random quirky puzzles, brawling through a hurricane of logical escapades and surpassing limits of your own intellectual prowess. Cryptocrux is the event where you pair up with your friends to take a logical reasoning and mind-wrecking test over the course of 2 days, spanning 3 rounds! With every round being unique, this event is sure to keep you on your toes!

Game Play

  • Participants must register themselves for CRYPTOCRUX under www.axisvnit.org before participating in any of the events.
  • There will be 3 rounds• Round 1 is Pre-Elimination Round, Round 2 is Elimination Round and Round 3 is Final.
  • All rounds are Pen and Paper based.
  • A Team can consist of maximum 2 members.
  • Round 1 will be of 1 hour.
  • Round 2 and Round 3 details will be provided to selected participants after the pre-elimination and elimination round respectively.
  • In case of any discrepancies, decisions of Event Manager will be final and binding.
  • NOTE: In case of any disputes, the decisions taken by the techNITi19 Executive Committee will be bound and final.

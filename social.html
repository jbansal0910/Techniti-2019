Eco Week

techNITi19’19 in collaboration with UN Environment organized an Eco Week campaign for World Environment Day on 5th June. Under this campaign, awareness about various technologies to curb plastic pollution was spread among people. This was done as the theme decided by UN this year was “Beat Plastic Pollution” and the host country was India. A Street Play was also conducted on Sunday 3rd June at Nagpur Central Mall at 4:30PM. The play mainly focused on the problems faced by the environment due to carelessness of humans. The performance was flawless and good number of audience was present at the moment as prior announcement was done at the mall. Social Entrepreneur Ms. Vrundan Bawankar who is currently the Executive Director at Gram Vikas Sanstha was invited.
